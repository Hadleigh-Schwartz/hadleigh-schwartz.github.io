I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at Columbia University, advised by {{site.content.xia}}, {{site.content.dan}}, and {{site.content.vishal}}. I am broadly interested in security and mobile systems. Currently, I'm examining ways to proactively protect videos from deepfake manipulation by injecting imperceptible acoustic or optical signals into the environment at recording time. These efforts build off of my experiences in optical sensing and communication. I'm a recipient of a Columbia SEAS Presidential Distinguished Fellowship.
